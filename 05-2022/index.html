Greetings

 

We're excited to introduce our first Immersive Retreat Program: 'Wild Spirit', taking place this October at the beautiful Klahoose Wilderness Resort in Desolation Sound, BC!

 

Wild Spirit is a celebration of Wilderness, Wellness, and Connection... an invitation to unplug and welcome the shifting seasons with intention. Over the 5-day retreat, expert facilitators will guide us through a holistic approach to personal wellness, connecting the mind, body and spirit through yoga, nutrition, outdoor adventure and ceremony.

 

Fall into the rhythm of nature in BC's pristine wilderness at this luxury off-grid resort, owned and operated by the original stewards of the land - the Klahoose First Nation.
